Ceylon Journal of Science

The Ceylon Journal of Science is an open access journal under Sri Lanka Journals Online (SLJOL) platform.

It accepts articles written in English in all areas of Sciences under five themes: Chemistry; Earth and Environment; Mathematics, Statistics and IT; Life Sciences and Physics. It is published quarterly in March, June, September and December each year. All articles published by the journal are deposited with Crossref.

Emotional Health

"Emotional Health" by Bob Johnson discuss what emotions are and how they cause social and mental diseases. This book assumes from the outset that emotions should be controlled by oneself rather than the other way around.

The author, Dr. Bob Johnson obtained his B.A. degree in Psychology (special) from the University of Peradeniya, his Ph.D degreefrom Deakin University, Australia.

The Happier Approach

This publication "The Happier Approach" by Jane Smith is focused on teaching the reader to be kind to themself, and in return be happier while still being able to accomplish their goals without hassle.

The author Ms. Jane Smith completed her B.Sc degree at the University of Peradeniya and continued her higher studies to obtain a Ms.Ed. from the University of Dayton, Ohio.
